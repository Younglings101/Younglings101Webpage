Start Coding

The program starts with 2 years of weekly classes to supplement school learning in Java.

If you don’t have this baseline you may need to complete a bridging course to get up to speed.

The most important things are a passion for tech and a drive to learn.

Solidify Learning

After passing your tests, you’ll move into CapaCiti for a 6-month course to solidify your learning in Java and JavaScript.

Thanks to Oracle and the Western Cape Government, the course is free to students.

Launch Your Career

You’ll finish your training with 6-months at an innovative company like Aliens, DVT, and more.

Then you’ll move into a 1 year learnership to work on a real project as a software developer.

You’ve made it! After the learnership you’ll gain employment with your company, move to another one, or start your own with support.

Grow as an IT Professional

You’ve made it! After the learnership you’ll gain employment with your company, move to another one, or start your own with support.

You’ll work with amazing mentors, contribute to various projects, and try out different techs on the bleeding edge of web design, mobile design, back-end programming, and more.
